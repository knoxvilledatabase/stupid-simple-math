# Stupid Simple Math

*A journey through the philosophy of mathematics from the eyes of an ADHD and dyslexic child.*

---

I remember like it was yesterday, the school called to notify my parents that there was a problem.

The diagnosis? Learning disabled.

I wrote this to explain why they were right.

## "He can't even grasp the number zero!"

The teacher screamed at my dad when he got upset.

I knew she was telling the truth, I thought to myself: 

"Why can't she explain how zero is multiple things?"

But I decided to keep my mouth shut as my father was already very upset.

## "He thinks 0 is 1!"

My dad sat there, jaw tight.

"If I picture a zero in my mind, how many symbols am I holding?"

But I continued staring at the floor. 

## "He thinks everything equals 1!!!"

The teacher threw her hands up.

I thought to myself: 

"Is a / b = c not one container divided by one container equals one container?"

I shook my head.


## "He told me that 0/0 is NOT undefined!"

I could see the disappointment in my father's eyes.

"How can a group of symbols be 'undefined'?" I thought. 

But again, I stayed quiet.


## "He thinks numbers can only become other numbers!"

My dad looked at me. I looked down and thought:

"How can symbols create anything other than symbols?"


## "He says nothing is something!"

The teacher paused. He's always a smartass!

"Could I take a picture of nothing?"
